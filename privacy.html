Privacy policy

Introduction:

This policy explains how we, SECP SDMS APP use the personal information which you provide to us when using our App.Please read this Privacy Policy and understand it before using our services.

Objective:
The main objective of this project is to develop Service Desk Management System to lodge query/complaint by using SECP SDMS .

Use of Collect Information:

The Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan has successfully launched its online Service Desk Management System fully integrated with a toll-free number and SMS service on June 1, 2017. SECP has now launched user friendly MOBILE APP for the users. Now, you can file your query/complaint by using SECP SDMS Mobile App at your convenience directly with relevant department. The new App provides the general public much convenience to lodge the new queries and complaints in short span of time. In addition to this , the user can also track the fate of their queries and complaints instantly. Moreover , you can also find the contact details of relevant company registration Office..

Changes to our Privacy Policy 
SECP SDMS reserves the right to alter all or any part of this Privacy Policy. Any changes thereto will be notified via the APP and, where appropriate, through e-mail notification or sms.
